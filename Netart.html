Net Art

A Place To Call Home is a text based game created through HTML coding, with all the illustrations being hand drawn on Adobe Procreate.
The concept all started with a drawing I did back in 2017. It was something that I had created a story behind and wanted to finally show others.
Players wake up and find themselves in an unknown area and try to make it back to where they call home, while also trying to remember what home is.
This is just the beginning and a sample of the story line I had planned out. There is more to come with this adventure of finding a place to call home.
