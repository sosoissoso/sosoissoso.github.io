DIY Photoshop


DIY Photoshop
Processing + JavaScript
2023

This is my verison of photoshop where the keys 1-0 act as predesigned stamp tools to make a forest scene.
The inspiration was to make elements for the tools to create a simple map.
The tools are as followed:
1- Clouds 2- Trees 3- Berry Tree
4- Bush 5- Berry Bush 6- Mushrooms
7- Pond 8- Wheat 9- Crops 0- Erase
G/g- Places a predesigned image F/f- Places a predesigned image S/s- Saves your image
