Journey Home

Where Am I?

You wake up face down in a puddle confused.

As you get yourself up you look around
to get a sense of your surroundings.

You stumble trying to stand up as the ground rumbles
due to the giants moving pass you.

You fall back down a few times, but eventually you were able to stand up.

You watch the giants head towards an opening
that leads to what seems to be underground.

What will you do next?

Lay back down and look at the sky.
Follow the giants.
