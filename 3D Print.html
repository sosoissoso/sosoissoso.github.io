3D PRINT



The character this piece is modeled after is Nyanko-Sensei, or Madara.
He is from an anime titled Natsume Yuujinchou. This piece signifies the cultural importance
and impact of Japanese animation. Although it may seem like just a cat,
it showcases how it had enough of a personal impact for it to even be created.
After my model was finished printing, I went in with my Utrecht acrylic paints and
added on more details to give the print more life and to resemble the character's design.
