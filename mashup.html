Mash Up

This piece once again discusses the topic of capitalism. I combined images of
three well known figures who seem to be pointing at each other,
because they are all the same. Each one has an abundance of money and
​control over the large mass of society. It calls to question just how much
​we as a society are controlled by these capitalists and we don't even fully realize it.
