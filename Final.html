Journey Home

3D Models and Prints

For my final project, I wanted to go back and worked on more on my text based game, Journey Home.
From my game, I chose elements from the story and used Maya Autodesk to create 3D models and FlashPrint to print them out. I chose to create
the main character of the game, the bench the character sits on, and the home he goes back to. Once I printed them out,
I used my Utrecht acrylic paints to match how I envisioned them to be. I also wanted to recreate scenes from my game
in a real world setting, so I set the prints in mutltiple settings to capture different tones of the game.
